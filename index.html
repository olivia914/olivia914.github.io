About Writing

As the editor at the Collegian, I know a thing or two about writing. It's taken years of practice to fine-tune my skills; now I have moved on to teaching others how to improve their own skills.

At the Collegian, we focus on answering this question: "What do the people need to know?"

Of course, the people don't always pay attention to what they need to know. We tell them in twenty words or fewer and still get questions from our readers.
